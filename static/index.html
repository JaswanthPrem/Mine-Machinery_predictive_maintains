IIT (B.H.U) Varanasi

The Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, commonly known as IIT (BHU), is a premier engineering institution located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 1919 as the Banaras Engineering College, it later became the Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University (IT-BHU) in 1968 and was granted the status of an IIT in 2012. IIT (BHU) Varanasi is renowned for its academic excellence in engineering, technology, and research. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines including Engineering, Sciences, Humanities, and Management. With a rich heritage and a focus on innovation and interdisciplinary learning, IIT (BHU) Varanasi continues to be a leading institution contributing significantly to the advancement of science and technology in India.

Board and pillar mining

Board and pillar mining

It is a specific underground mining method used to extract coal or other minerals that are deposited in seams or veins. This mining technique is characterized by the creation of a network of rooms (or panels) separated by pillars of coal or rock that support the overlying strata. It is widely used in coal mining due to its adaptability to varying geological conditions and deposit characteristics.

Longwall mining

Longwall mining

It is a highly efficient underground mining method used primarily for extracting coal. It involves the use of a specialized machine called a longwall shearer to cut and extract coal along a continuous horizontal face of the coal seam.
Longwall mining is known for its high productivity and relatively low operating costs compared to traditional room-and-pillar mining methods.

Open-cast mining"

Open-cast mining"

Its (also known as open-pit mining), which is a surface mining technique used to extract minerals or resources from the earth's surface. This method is commonly used for mining coal, ores (like copper, gold, iron), and other materials that are located near the surface and can be extracted through an open excavation.
